Shaders Practice Project In Unity

Just wanted to log the progress of myself learning shaders in Unity. I am not experienced with art techniques or gfx very much.


Here is what I started with. I just used a free .fbx model on the Unity Asset Store.

As you can see, the models colors are very dull and just don't stand out well in the game. So I wanted to improve this.

I went ahead and experimented with Unitys URP. Here is the first product!

With these first changes, you can see I improved the blurryness of the model, as well as the very dull color! I was happy to see results. Next I wanted to see if I could make the model look more like an anime.

I added a small outline on the character model, and changed her skin tone a little bit, but I was not very happy with much on this version except her eyes. I did a great job on the eye textures!

At this point I had to learn shaders, or at least how to apply shaders. So I gave it a try:

What an improvement since the start! The character now stands out even in a colorful world, and has a cool anime outline too!

I am not an artist, I am a programmer. So this is where my journey stops for now. I plan to apply this to my Unity MMORPG Project to really make it pop! Thanks for reading!
